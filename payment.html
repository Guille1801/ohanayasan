How to order?

Please send us your order by e-mail 3~2 days before you want the flowers delivered. We'll send you a reply by mail to confirm.

Delivery charge and tax are included in the price.

Ordering is easy!

1. Order Number and Price

Mention the price range and note that in summer (June to September), flowers are delivered by refrigerator car with a size limit, so no arrangements or bouquets over 10000 JPY.

2. Provide Your Name, Address, and Telephone Number

Ensure to provide accurate contact information to facilitate the delivery process.

3. Delivery Date and Time

Specify your preferred delivery time and mention the 'Five Time-Zone Delivery Service'.

4. Delivery Address

Ensure the delivery address is correct before sending the order.

5. Message Card

If sending a message by email, they will add an original card with your message; you can choose a card you like.

Note:

From 1st June, 2023, if you request a change of delivery address due to unknown address or moving, all freight charges will be cash on delivery.
The consignee will be responsible for the cost. Thank you for your understanding.
