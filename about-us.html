Our Services

We are a Japan flower online delivery offers beautifully crafted floral arrangements, roses, bouquets, wedding decorations, and gourmet baskets for any occasion.

Who we are?

We are owners of a flower shop in Tokyo. Since April 2000 we have expanded our business, and have started taking orders from overseas for deliveries within Japan. We ourselves are responsible for the selection, arrangement, and delivery of the flowers at local cost (that is, at prices offered to local customers in Tokyo). Please contact us for your next special occasion.We hope our flowers will bring happiness to you and your loved ones.
